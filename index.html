The Solution

Not another carpool app.
Commute infrastructure.

Three things Indian carpooling has never had together: portable peer trust, recurring scheduling, and a guaranteed fallback. SaathiSafar delivers all three.

SaathiScore
A portable trust score built from Aadhaar KYC, ride history, punctuality rate, employer email, and mutual connections. Replaces the need for institutional gatekeeping — any commuter can earn trust from their own behaviour.
Core differentiator
SafeSwitch Guarantee
If a pool cancels within 90 minutes of departure, SafeSwitch auto-triggers: alternate pool → transit route → subsidised ride-hail. A ₹30 reliability credit is applied automatically. Your morning never fails — guaranteed.
No competitor offers this
Recurring Schedule
Set your route and timing once. The pool auto-confirms every morning. No daily rebooking, no app-checking, no friction. Designed for the 5-day weekly commute, not one-off trips.
Set once, runs forever
WhatsApp Group Import
Users paste an existing colony carpool or office commuter WhatsApp group link. SaathiSafar maps members to corridors and creates a formalised, insured pool in under 3 minutes. Solves cold start immediately.
Cold-start solved
Women's Safety Stack
Women-only pool mode, live trip sharing to a trusted contact, route deviation alert at 500m, in-app SOS with Suraksha API integration, and a verified women driver badge tier. Safety designed in — not bolted on.
Largest underserved segment
Driver Reliability Deposit
Drivers hold ₹200–300/month in escrow. Repeated last-minute cancellations deduct from this deposit — funding the SafeSwitch guarantee. Strong reliability earns it back as a monthly bonus. Incentives aligned by design.
Self-funding guarantee

Why Us

Four unfair
advantages.

Trust architecture, not just an app
The SaathiScore compounds with every ride — creating a switching cost that pure ride-hail platforms can't replicate. A user with 50 rides and a 4.9 score isn't switching to a competitor that starts them at zero. This moat deepens automatically over time with no additional investment.
Distribution via existing behaviour
The WhatsApp group import doesn't just solve cold start — it means our distribution channel is the social graph our users already maintain. Every new colony carpool group that imports becomes a self-contained acquisition event. We don't buy users; we formalise existing communities.
Regulatory positioning, not exposure
We structure payments as peer cost-sharing, not fares — consistent with how Indian courts have treated similar arrangements. We're simultaneously engaging MoRTH's PM Gati Shakti regulatory sandbox to position SaathiSafar as infrastructure, not a taxi aggregator. Legal risk managed from day one.
Tier 2 cities are our home turf
Our pilot cities — Chandigarh, Indore — are the template for 40+ cities with 500K–3M population, functional bus networks, and zero digital transit layer. We understand these markets deeply. We're not retrofitting a Bengaluru playbook. We built this for Indore first.
